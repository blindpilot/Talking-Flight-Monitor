UPON ENTERING CABIN
1. Airplane Weight and Balance - CHECKED
2. Close and secure the cabin door
3. Control Wheel Lock - REMOVE
4. Controls - FREE and CORRECT
5. Ignition and Avionics - OFF
6. Master Switch - ON
7. Check Fuel Quantity
8. Fuel Selector - FULLEST TANK
9. Flaps - EXTEND then RETRACT
10. Elevator Trim - SET for takeoff
11. Beacon - ON
