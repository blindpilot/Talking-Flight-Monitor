ENGINE STARTING

1. Throttle - CRACKED 
2. Mixture - OUT
3. Prop Control - IN
4. Master Switch - ON
5. Beacon - ON
6. Cowl Flaps - OPEN
NOTE:  Skip to step 10 for a WARM Engine
7. Fuel Pump - ON
8. Mixture - RICH until stable fuel flow for 3-5 seconds, then set to IDLE CUTOFF
9. Fuel Pump - OFF
10. Area - CLEAR
11. Ignition Switch - START
12. Mixture - ADVANCE
13. Oil Pressure - CHECK and Amps - POSITIVE
14. Navigation Lights - ON as required
15. Avionics - ON 
16. Radios - ON
17. Flaps - RETRACT
