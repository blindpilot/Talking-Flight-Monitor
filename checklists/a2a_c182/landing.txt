LANDING
1. Airspeed - 70-80 kts (flaps UP)
2. Wing Flaps - AS DESIRED    
(0-10 - 140 kts)    
(10-20 - 120 kts)    
(Full - 100 kts)
3. Airspeed - 60-70 kts (flaps DOWN)
4. Touchdown - MAIN WHEELS FIRST
5. Landing Roll - GENTLY LOWER NOSE
6. Braking - MINIMUM REQUIRED

AFTER LANDING
1. Wing Flaps - UP 
2. Cowl Flaps - OPEN
