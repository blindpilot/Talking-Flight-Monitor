ENGINE STARTING

1. Throttle - CRACKED 
2. Mixture - IDLE
3. Master Switch - ON 
4. Beacon - ON
NOTE:  Skip to step 9 for a WARM Engine
5. Auxiliary Fuel Pump Switch - ON
6. Mixture - RICH until stable fuel flow for    
3-5 seconds, then set to IDLE CUTOFF
7. Auxiliary Fuel Pump - OFF
8. Propeller Area - CLEAR
9. Ignition Switch - START
10. Mixture - ADVANCE
11. Oil Pressure - CHECK
12. Navigation Lights - ON as required
13. Avionics - ON
14. Radios - ON
15. Flaps - RETRACT
