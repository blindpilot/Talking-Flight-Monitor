BEFORE TAKEOFF
1. Controls - FREE and CORRECT
2. Fuel Selectors - DESIRED TANK
3. Fuel Pump - OFF
4. Mixture - RICH or as req for elevation
5. Prop - FULL IN
6. Engine Gauges - CHECK
7. Trim Tab - NEUTRAL
8. Flaps - SET
9. Cowl flaps - OPEN
10. Doors and Windows - SECURE
11. Slowly advance throttle
12. Manifold, FF, RPM - CHECK
